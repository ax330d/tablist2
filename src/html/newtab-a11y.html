Keyboard Controls

  • Navigate:
    Use Shift + Arrow Up/Down or Tab to move focus between tabs and group headers, skipping collapsed groups.
  • Tab Options:
    Press Arrow Right on a tab to open options, Arrow Left to close and focus the tab. Use Tab to cycle through options.
  • Activate Tab:
    Press Arrow Left to focus a tab's favicon, then Enter to switch to it.
  • Group Toggle:
    Press Enter on a group header to collapse or expand it.
  • Change Theme:
    Pres Arrow Right or Arrow Left to switch themes when focused on the theme button.
  • Tab Actions:
    Use x to close, m to discard, or r to reload a focused tab.
  • Drag-and-Drop:
    Press d to start dragging, Arrow Up/Down to move, Enter to drop, Escape to cancel.
  • Dialogs:
    Press s to start selecting, Enter to toggle tabs, s again to finish, Escape to cancel. Use Tab or arrows to choose options, Enter to confirm, Escape to cancel in dialogs.
