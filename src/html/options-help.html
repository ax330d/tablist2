TabList Options Help

  • Show full title/URL:
    Toggle to display the complete title/URL of each tab instead of truncating it.
  • Title/info font size:
    Adjust the font size (in rem) of tab titles or timestamps with URL, from 0 to 5. Increase for readability or decrease to fit more tabs on screen.
  • Hide TabList:
    Toggle to exclude the TabList tab itself from the list.
  • Keep one TabList:
    Ensure only one TabList tab remains open at a time. When opening new tab, current TabList will close all other TabLists.
  • Sync group folding:
    Synchronize the collapsed/expanded state of tab groups between TabList and your browser for a consistent experience.
  • Interface style:
    Choose between "Round" or "Semi-square" styles for TabList's appearance. Pick what suits your taste!
  • Light/Dark Theme:
    Customize the background gradient for light and dark modes. Set the angle (0-360Â°) and "From" and "To" gradient colors (via picker or hex) to match your vibe. Themes follow your selection from the main page (Light, Dark, or System).
  • Restore Defaults:
    Click to reset all settings to their original values.
